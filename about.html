About domain

Crafting intimate luxury with passion, precision, and artistry since 2010.

Our Story

Where Elegance Meets Intimacy

domain was born from a single conviction: that every woman deserves to feel extraordinary in her most private moments. Founded in 2010, we began as a small atelier devoted to handcrafted lingerie using only the finest silks, French laces, and sustainably sourced materials.

Over more than a decade, we have grown into a trusted luxury brand, serving clients across Russia and beyond — yet our commitment to individual craftsmanship and intimate personalisation has never wavered. Each piece is a wearable work of art.

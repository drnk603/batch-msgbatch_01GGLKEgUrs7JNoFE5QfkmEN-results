Privacy Policy

At domain, your privacy is as important as your comfort. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Last Updated:

domain ("we", "our", "us") operates the website at domain.com (the "Service"). We are committed to protecting and respecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy sets out the basis on which any personal data we collect from you, or that you provide to us, will be processed.

Please read the following carefully to understand our practices regarding your personal data and how we will treat it. By using our website, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this policy.

3. Personal Data We Collect

We may collect and process the following categories of personal data:

3.1 Data You Provide Directly

  • Identity Data: First name, last name, title
  • Contact Data: Email address, telephone number, billing and shipping address
  • Transaction Data: Details of purchases, payment method (we do not store full card details)
  • Communication Data: Messages sent via contact forms, emails, or customer support enquiries
  • Account Data: Username, password (encrypted), preferences, and saved items

3.2 Data Collected Automatically

  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type and version, operating system, device identifiers
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent, links clicked, referral sources
  • Cookie Data: Data collected through cookies and similar tracking technologies (see Section 8)
  • Location Data: Approximate geolocation derived from IP address

3.3 Data From Third Parties

  • Analytics providers (e.g., Google Analytics)
  • Advertising and social media platforms
  • Payment processors (e.g., Stripe, PayPal)

We process your personal data for the following purposes, relying on the legal bases as stated under GDPR Article 6:

5. Sharing Your Data With Third Parties

We do not sell your personal data. We may share your data with the following categories of third parties for the purposes described above:

5.1 Service Providers

  • Payment processors for secure transaction handling
  • Logistics and courier companies for order delivery
  • Email service providers for transactional and marketing communications
  • Cloud infrastructure and hosting providers
  • Customer support software providers

5.2 Analytics & Advertising Partners

  • Google LLC (Google Analytics, Google Ads) — subject to Google's Privacy Policy
  • Meta Platforms Inc. (Facebook Pixel) — subject to Meta's Data Policy

5.3 Legal Disclosures

We may disclose your personal data if required to do so by law, or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., courts, regulatory bodies).

5.4 International Transfers

Some of our third-party service providers are based outside of the European Economic Area (EEA). Where we transfer data internationally, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, such as Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by the European Commission, or an adequacy decision.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes for which it was collected, including legal, accounting, and reporting obligations:

  • Transaction and order data: 7 years (legal/accounting requirement)
  • Account data: Duration of the account, plus 2 years after closure
  • Marketing consent records: Until consent is withdrawn, plus 12 months
  • Customer support communications: 3 years
  • Analytics data: Up to 26 months (anonymised thereafter)
  • Cookie data: Session cookies expire on browser close; persistent cookies per their defined lifespan

After the applicable retention period, data is securely deleted or anonymised.

7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies (web beacons, pixels, local storage) to enhance your browsing experience, analyse site traffic, and serve relevant advertising.

7.1 Types of Cookies We Use

  • Strictly Necessary Cookies: Essential for the website to function (e.g., shopping cart, login session). These cannot be disabled.
  • Performance & Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with the site (e.g., Google Analytics).
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences such as language or region.
  • Targeting & Advertising Cookies: Used to deliver personalised ads based on your browsing behaviour.

7.2 Managing Cookies

You can manage your cookie preferences at any time via our Cookie Settings panel or by adjusting your browser settings. Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of our website.

For full details, please read our Cookie Policy.

8. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

8.1 Rights Under GDPR (EEA Residents)

  • Right of Access (Art. 15): Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification (Art. 16): Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to Erasure (Art. 17): Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten"), subject to legal retention requirements.
  • Right to Restrict Processing (Art. 18): Request that we limit how we use your data in certain circumstances.
  • Right to Data Portability (Art. 20): Receive your data in a structured, commonly used format.
  • Right to Object (Art. 21): Object to processing based on legitimate interests or for direct marketing purposes.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent (Art. 7(3)): Withdraw previously given consent at any time without affecting prior processing.
  • Right to Lodge a Complaint: File a complaint with your national supervisory authority (e.g., Roskomnadzor for Russia, or a relevant EU Data Protection Authority).

8.2 Rights Under CCPA (California Residents)

  • Right to Know: Request disclosure of the categories and specific pieces of personal information we have collected.
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of personal information we have collected, subject to exceptions.
  • Right to Opt-Out: We do not sell personal information. However, you may opt out of sharing for targeted advertising.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your CCPA rights.

9. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for all data transmitted to and from our website
  • Encrypted storage of sensitive data (passwords, payment tokens)
  • Access controls and role-based permissions for staff
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Incident response procedures compliant with GDPR Article 33 (72-hour breach notification)

While we take every reasonable precaution, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We encourage you to use strong passwords and keep your account credentials confidential.

10. Children's Privacy

Our website and services are intended for adults aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect personal data from individuals under the age of 16 (or the applicable age of digital consent in their jurisdiction). If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected such data, we will delete it promptly.

If you believe a minor has provided us with personal information, please contact us at requests@domain.com.

11. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party sites you visit.

12. Automated Decision-Making & Profiling

We may use automated tools to personalise product recommendations and targeted advertising based on your browsing and purchase history. This processing is based on consent or legitimate interest and does not produce significant legal effects without human review. You may object to such processing at any time.

13. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, legal requirements, or services. We will notify you of material changes by posting the updated policy on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date. For significant changes, we may also notify you by email.

We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your data.
